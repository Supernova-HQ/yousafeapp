How YouSafe Works

Shake to alert!

In moments when you feel threatened or unsafe, you can easily trigger an alert by simply shaking your phone. This discreet gesture ensures that you can activate the alert function without drawing unwanted attention to yourself, providing a silent call for help.

Instant Notifcation

Once the alert is activated, an SMS or WhatsApp message is immediately sent to up to five pre-selected contacts, containing your current location. This ensures that your trusted contacts are instantly informed of your situation and can take appropriate action to assist you.

Peace of mind

Knowing that your emergency contacts are instantly notified provides unparalleled peace of mind. They receive precise location data and are aware of your situation, allowing them to respond quickly and effectively. Whether they choose to contact you directly, alert local authorities, or come to your aid themselves, you are never alone in a moment of need.

Our Features

Quick set up

Setting up your emergency contacts and configuring your YouSafe app is a straightforward and hassle-free process. With a user-friendly interface, you can easily add and manage your emergency contacts during the initial sign-up process, ensuring that your safety network is ready to go in minutes.

Discreet Activation

The discreet activation feature is designed to help you seek assistance without drawing attention. By simply shaking your phone, you can send an alert silently. This is especially useful in situations where making a phone call or sending a message might not be safe or feasible.

Reliable location-Sharing

YouSafe uses advanced GPS technology to provide precise and accurate location data in every alert message. This ensures that your emergency contacts know exactly where you are, allowing them to find you quickly and efficiently. The accuracy of the location data is critical in emergency situations, where every second counts.

Frequently Asked Questions

These are our frequently asked questions by customers
Who is YouSafe?

YouSafe is a personal safety app designed to empower women and families by providing a quick and discreet way to alert trusted contacts in case of danger. By simply shaking their phone, users can send an alert to five pre-selected contacts.

How does YouSafe works?

In case of danger, users can shake their phone to activate the YouSafe app. This action sends an emergency alert to five pre-selected contacts with the user's current location. The app can also alert local law enforcement if needed.

Who can use YouSafe?

YouSafe is designed for women, families and the elderly who want to enhance their personal safety and any other form of accident.

Can YouSafe be used offline

YouSafe requires an internet connection to send alerts and share your location with your emergency contacts. However, you can still access some offline safety features like sending emergency messages on feature phones.
